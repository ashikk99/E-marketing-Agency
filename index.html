Google Ads Camoagin

A Google Ads campaign is an organized set of advertisements run through Google’s advertising platform (Google Ads), designed to achieve specific marketing objectives like increasing website traffic, generating leads, boosting product sales, or promoting brand awareness. Web Analytics

Web analytics is the process of collecting, measuring, analyzing, and reporting data related to the behavior of visitors on a website. The goal of web analytics is to understand how users interact with the website and to use that information to improve user experience, optimize the site’s performance, and ultimately drive business outcomes such as conversions, sales, or lead generation..
